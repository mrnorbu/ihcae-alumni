IHCAE Alumni Application Tutorial

A step-by-step guide for basic users, registration, and administrators.

1. The Guest Experience (Without Logging In)

Before you even create an account, the IHCAE Alumni App offers valuable information to the public. When you navigate to the main website, you land on the Homepage.

The Homepage

As a guest visiting the site, you will see a public landing page. Here, you have options to join the community or sign in. The navigation bar provides access to public sections.

Figure 1: The main homepage displaying the login area and public navigation.

News & Events

Clicking on "News & Events" in the navigation bar takes you to the public notice board. You can see upcoming events, such as reunions and training programs, as well as the latest success stories without needing an account.

Figure 2: The public News & Events page.

2. Registration: Joining the Network

To view other alumni and access the Directory, you must become a registered member.

How to Register

  • On the top right of the Homepage, click the Join Network or Register button.
  • You will be presented with a registration form. You must fill in all required fields:
    • Personal Information: First Name, Last Name.
    • Contact Details: A valid Email Address and a secure Password.
  • Once filled out, click the submit button.
Figure 3: The registration form where new alumni provide their details.

What happens after I register?

For security and verification purposes, your account goes into a "Pending Approval" state. An administrator must manually review your details and approve your account. You will be able to log in and view the Directory only after this approval is granted.

3. The Administrator Experience

Administrators have special privileges to manage users and content. This section details the various admin dashboards.

The Admin Dashboard

After logging in with admin credentials, click on the Admin link in the navigation bar. This takes you to the central console overview.

  • Overview Panel: View total registered alumni, pending accounts, and active users.
  • Quick Actions: Easily jump to user management or content creation.
Figure 4: The Admin Dashboard showing user statistics and quick actions.

Managing the Directory (Alumni Hub)

By clicking on Alumni & User Management (or the Alumni Hub), admins can oversee the entire directory and manage approvals.

  • Pending Approvals: View a list of new users who have recently registered. You can verify their identity and click Approve.
  • Directory: Search for specific alumni and view their full profiles.
Figure 5: The Alumni Hub for managing user approvals and the directory.

Content Management (News & Events)

Administrators also have the ability to create, edit, or delete the News and Events that are displayed to the public.

  • Navigate to the News & Events section inside the Admin Console.
  • You can add new success stories or schedule upcoming events from this screen.
Figure 6: The Content Management page for publishing news and events.
